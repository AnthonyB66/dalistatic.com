To visit us

You can reach Perpignan by plane (Air France), train (TGV - SNCF) or car (A9 from Narbonne or Le Perthus Spanish Border).

From Aéroport International de Perpignan-Rivesaltes :

Taxis (about 15 euros , 30 minutes long).

Schuttle-bus "Courriers Catalans" (about 5 euros, 1 hour to reach the Perpignan Railway Station); bus stops at "Place de Catalogne" and at the railway station. Shuttle available one hour before the first flight to one hour after the last flight.

From Perpignan Railway Station :

Taxis (about 10 euros, 10 minutes long)

Bus (about 1 euro, 15 minutes long). Every 5 minutes from 6:30 AM to 8:00 PM from monday to friday. Go to Place de Catalogne: 5 minutes walking or bus no 2 or 19. Pick the bus no 8 at Place de Catalogne, direction "Université", stop at "Campus" (in front of a Proxy shop). Cross Avenue Paul Alduy to come into the campus

From Autoroute A9 :

Exit "Perpignan Sud": follow "Gerone" until the the 3rd round-about then follow "Université"

Exit "Perpignan Nord": follow "Barcelone" through the town centre then Moulin-à-Vent/Université;

From the Campus Main Entrance ("Entrée Principale" at the bottom of the campus map) :

We are located at the 1st floor of the batiment B (in the center of the campus map).May be useful: maps of Perpignan and the Campus
